About the App

Charity A Day is a daily-impact mobile application designed to blend learning, altruism, and brand sponsorship into a simple, powerful routine. Each day, the app features a different vetted nonprofit organization. Whether the trivia answer is right or wrong, a real donation is made by a corporate sponsor — never the user. 100% free. 100% ad-free. Always impactful.

How It Works

  1. Open the app daily to see a trivia question.
  2. Answer it — and trigger a sponsor-funded donation.
  3. Learn about a new nonprofit every day.

Our Work

We feature a new nonprofit every day — from climate action and education to health and humanitarian aid. Our partners range from grassroots changemakers to well-established organizations. We spotlight them with care, giving them donations and visibility they might otherwise not receive.
